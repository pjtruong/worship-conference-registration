About Worship Conf

The beautiful city of Irvine, CA will be the host city for Worship Music Conf!

Explore the future of Worship Ministry with a lineup of annointed Christian leaders. Discover the power and authority nested in you as a worshiper.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international church community and spent entire day in worship, prayer, and ministry? with worship teams from Bethel, IHOP, Hillsong, and many more!

This conference is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to this life changing day.

Speakers

Carl Lentz

Carl and his wife, Laura, are Lead Pastors at Hillsong Church NYC. While pastoring his home church is Carl's main priority, he also travels globally to speak at conferences & events. Carl and Laura have three beautiful children.

Brian & Jenn Johnson

Brian and Jenn Johnson are the founders of Bethel Music & oversee the worship department at Bethel Church in Redding, CA. They've written songs such as, "One Thing Remains," "We Will Not Be Shaken" & "Have It All."

Lou Engle

Lou Engle is a revivalist and co-founder of TheCall. Lou's passion has been to call believers into radical consecration through prayer, fasting, and acts of justice. Lou is a part of the leadership team at the Pasadena International House of Prayer.

Jeremy Riddle

Jeremy Riddle is a worship leader and songwriter at Bethel Church in Redding, CA. Jeremy is most known for his songs, "This Is Amazing Grace" and "Fall Afresh." Jeremy carries a passion for Godâ€™s presence.

Steffany Gretzinger

Steffany Gretzinger is a worship leader and songwriter at Bethel Church in Redding, CA. Steffany carries a powerful prophetic voice and pursues the Lord with raw passion. Her songs include "Closer" and "Letting Go" and her solo album, "The Undoing."

Cory Asbury

Cory Asbury is a worship leader and songwriter who joined the Bethel Music Artist Collective in 2015. His song "Son was Lifted Up" has become a popular worship song. Cory and his family live in Colorado Springs, CO and are worship leaders at New Life Church.
